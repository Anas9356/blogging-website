What is Web Design?

Web design refers to the design of websites that are displayed on the internet.

A web designer works on the appearance, layout, and, in some cases, content of a website. Appearance, for instance, relates to the colors, font, and images used. Layout refers to how information is structured and categorized. A good web design is easy to use, aesthetically pleasing, and suits the user group and brand of the website. Two of the most common methods for designing websites that work well both on desktop and mobile are responsive and adaptive design.
